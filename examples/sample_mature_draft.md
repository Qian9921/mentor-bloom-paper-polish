# Abstract

This paper presents a very novel integrated positioning framework for urban navigation. It is worth noting that the method improves localization accuracy under difficult conditions. The proposed system uses GNSS and inertial measurements to provide robust results.

# Introduction

Urban navigation remains challenging because signal blockage and multipath effects degrade positioning quality. Many existing methods improve performance, but they do not always remain stable in dense urban canyons.

In this work, we propose a learning-assisted integrated positioning framework. The framework combines GNSS observations with inertial cues and a neural estimator. Experimental results show better performance than several baselines.
